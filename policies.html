Code of Conduct

We are committed to a safe, professional, and respectful environment for all participants—attendees, faculty, exhibitors, organisers, and staff. Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or disruptive behaviour is not acceptable.

Reporting: If you experience or witness misconduct, please contact the organising team via the Contact page.
Enforcement: Organisers may take appropriate action, including removal from the event.

Disclosure & scientific integrity

Presenters should disclose relevant financial and non-financial relationships related to the work being presented to support transparency and trust in scientific communication.

Privacy

We collect registration and (if applicable) abstract submission information only for conference administration (confirmations, badges, program communication, certificates). We do not sell attendee data.

Photography & media

By attending, participants may be photographed/recorded in common areas and sessions for conference documentation. If you prefer not to appear, please inform the organisers at registration.

Refund / cancellation

Cancellation requests must be submitted by email. Refund rules and cut-off dates will be published once registration categories/pricing are finalised.
