Use this structure to drive submissions by clarifying what happens after submission. Replace numbers and deadlines with your final decisions.

Submission → Review → Presentation

  1. All abstracts are reviewed for relevance, clarity, and completeness.
  2. Accepted abstracts are assigned as e-Posters or Posters.
  3. Top posters are shortlisted for “Award Category”.
  4. Top award winners are invited for Oral Presentations.

Suggested judging rubric

  • Clinical relevance / impact
  • Methodological rigour
  • Clarity of results and conclusion
  • Originality / novelty
  • Quality of discussion and limitations
