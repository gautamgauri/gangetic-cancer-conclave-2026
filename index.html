About the conclave

Gangetic Cancer Conclave (Chapter II) is designed as an interdisciplinary forum for oncology professionals—built to strengthen cross-specialty collaboration and translate evidence into high-value, patient-centred clinical practice.

The programme emphasizes precision medicine, emerging technologies, and evolving therapeutic paradigms—paired with ethically grounded, compassionate decision-making aligned with patient values, dignity, and quality of life.
