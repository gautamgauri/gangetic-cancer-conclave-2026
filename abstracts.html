Abstracts & Presentations

Publish your submission link, rules, and timelines here. A simple ladder (Poster → Award → Oral) often increases submissions.

Recommended abstract format

  • Title
  • Background
  • Methods
  • Results
  • Conclusion
  • Keywords (3–5)
  • Disclosure statement

Suggested length: 300 words (easy for Google Forms).

Suggested topics

Medical · Radiation · Surgical · Pediatric · Hemato-oncology · Psycho-oncology · Radiology · Molecular oncology · Genetics · Pathology · Pharmacy · Research · Supportive care · QI/implementation.

How to submit

  1. Prepare your abstract in the format above.
  2. Submit via the Abstract Submission Form/Portal (add link here).
  3. You'll receive a confirmation message.
  4. Accepted abstracts will be assigned to oral/poster formats based on review.
