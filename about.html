Gangetic Cancer Conclave — Chapter II

Theme: Cancer Care Across South Asia: Innovation, Access, and Pragmatic Excellence

Conference overview

Gangetic Cancer Conclave (Chapter II) is conceived as a multidisciplinary forum for oncology professionals—built to strengthen cross-specialty collaboration and translate evidence into high-value, patient-centred clinical practice.

The programme emphasizes precision medicine, emerging technologies, and evolving therapeutic paradigms—paired with ethically grounded, compassionate decision-making aligned with patient values, dignity, and quality of life.

Dates & venue

Dates: 4th & 5th April 2026
Start: 09:00 AM
Venue: Welcomhotel by ITC Hotels & Convention Centre, Bodh Gaya, India

Download brochure (PDF)

Who should attend

Medical, radiation, surgical, pediatric, hemato-oncology, psycho-oncology; radiology, genetics, pathology, pharmacy, research, molecular oncology; trainees and medical students; and allied care teams.
