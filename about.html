Conference overview

Gangetic Cancer Conclave (Chapter II) is a multidisciplinary forum for oncology professionals—built to strengthen cross-specialty collaboration and translate evidence into high-value, patient-centred clinical practice across South Asia.

The programme emphasizes precision medicine, emerging technologies, and evolving therapeutic paradigms—paired with ethically grounded, compassionate decision-making aligned with patient values, dignity, and quality of life.

Learning format

Participants will engage through expert-led plenaries, in-depth panel discussions, interactive workshops, and case-based learning drawn from diverse healthcare environments.

Who should attend

Medical, radiation, surgical, pediatric, hemato-oncology, psycho-oncology; radiology, genetics, pathology, pharmacy, research, molecular oncology; trainees and medical students; and allied care teams.
