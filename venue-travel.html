Getting there

  • By air: Gaya International Airport is the nearest airport; plan a road transfer to Bodh Gaya.
  • By rail: Gaya Junction is the nearest major railhead; continue by road to Bodh Gaya.
  • By road: Bodh Gaya is connected by road from Gaya and regional hubs; allow buffer time.

Local transport & stay

Taxis and autos are commonly used for last-mile travel. If travelling in groups, consider arranging pickups in advance. If you prefer minimal commute, consider staying at the conference venue hotel (subject to availability).
